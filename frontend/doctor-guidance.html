Health Safety Guidance

When Should You Visit a Doctor?

Some symptoms can be managed at home, while others require professional medical attention.

🤒

High Fever

Visit a doctor if fever lasts more than 2 days or becomes very high.

😮‍💨

Breathing Problems

Difficulty breathing may require immediate medical attention.

🤕

Severe Headache

Repeated or intense headaches should be checked by a doctor.

🤢

Vomiting

Continuous vomiting or dehydration can become dangerous.

💔

Chest Pain

Chest pain should never be ignored and may require emergency care.

🩺

Persistent Symptoms

If symptoms continue for many days, seek professional advice.
